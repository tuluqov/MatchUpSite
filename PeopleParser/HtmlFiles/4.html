            Famous People Born In April

However, they can be revengeful when people or circumstances turn against them. They are equally pleasant and vicious and can be generous, as well as aggressive. However, they are some of the best people to be around. They are exactly the ones you can look up to when faced with difficulties.  Individuals born in this month are controlled by the dual forces of the zodiac sign, Aries and the planet of Mars. Known as the ‘The Lord of War’, the planet Mars makes the people under its influence, impatient and destructive. These individuals are born leaders and are usually attracted to sports, engineering and the military services as career choices. They are always active and charged, the quality very few people possess and even if they do, not to such a degree. They must try to control their emotions and need to learn to be patient.
